Privacy Policy

Important Notice: RWBTRD is a completely offline application. All your fitness data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how RWBTRD ("we," "our," or "the application") handles information in our fitness tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal fitness data.

RWBTRD is designed as a local-first application, meaning all data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or back it up.

2. Information Collection and Storage

Local Data Storage: All fitness data you enter into RWBTRD, including exercise records, quantities, units, notes, and dates, is stored exclusively on your personal device. This includes:

  • Exercise activities and repetitions
  • Duration, distance, and weight measurements
  • Calorie tracking data
  • Personal notes and comments
  • Date and time of your workouts
  • Generated statistics and trend analysis

3. No Data Transmission

RWBTRD operates entirely offline. The application does not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Require an internet connection for core functionality
  • Use cloud storage for your fitness data
  • Share information with third parties
  • Collect analytics or usage statistics

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is primarily maintained through your device's built-in security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device security features (passcode, biometrics)
  • Regularly backing up your device data

5. Data Backup and Export

RWBTRD may offer optional features for data backup and export. If you choose to use these features:

  • You maintain full control over where your data is backed up
  • Export functions will require your explicit permission
  • Backup locations (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.) are governed by their respective privacy policies

6. Permissions

RWBTRD may request certain device permissions for enhanced functionality:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your fitness data locally
  • Optional Permissions: Any additional permissions would be clearly explained and require your consent

No permissions are required for the basic functionality of recording and analyzing your fitness data.

7. Children's Privacy

RWBTRD does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app operates entirely locally and doesn't transmit data, any information entered by children remains solely on the device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the application and the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy will be updated. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
